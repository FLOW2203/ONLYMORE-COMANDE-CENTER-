# DOJUKU SHINGI - Personas

## Persona 1: Le Parent Rust Belt

### Profil

- **Archetype**: Parent working-class dans une ville industrielle en reconversion (Detroit, Pittsburgh, Cleveland, Gary)
- **Age**: 30-45 ans
- **Contexte**: Budget serre, cherche des activites educatives abordables pour ses enfants, sensible a la diversite culturelle
- **Digital**: Smartphone Android, Facebook, YouTube

### Douleur Principale

Peu d'options educatives abordables et culturellement riches. Les jeux video mainstream sont violents ou superficiels. Veut du contenu qui enseigne des valeurs sans etre ennuyeux.

### Objectif

Trouver une activite educative que ses enfants adorent ET qui transmet des valeurs de respect, discipline et ouverture culturelle.

### Objections Courantes

1. "Is this just another fighting game?"
2. "My kids won't play something 'educational'."
3. "Can I trust the cultural representation?"

### Message Cle

"DOJUKU SHINGI teaches your kids discipline, respect, and cultural wisdom through the art of martial arts, not the violence. And they'll love it."

### Canal Prefere

- Facebook community groups
- YouTube reviews / let's play
- Word of mouth (school, dojo, community center)

## Persona 2: Le Senior Pratiquant

### Profil

- **Archetype**: Retraite ou pre-retraite, ex-pratiquant d'arts martiaux, souvent instructeur ou mentor
- **Age**: 55-70 ans
- **Contexte**: Nostalgique de sa pratique, veut transmettre, se reconnait dans les personnages de maitres ages
- **Digital**: iPad/tablette, email, certains sur Facebook

### Douleur Principale

Sentiment que la sagesse martiale se perd. Les jeunes veulent du MMA violent, pas de la philosophie. Les dojos ferment dans les petites villes.

### Objectif

Trouver un medium moderne qui honore la tradition et permet de transmettre aux petits-enfants.

### Objections Courantes

1. "Video games can't teach real martial arts."
2. "Is this respectful of the traditions?"
3. "I'm not good with technology."

### Message Cle

"DOJUKU SHINGI is the bridge between your wisdom and the next generation. The masters in this game are you."

### Canal Prefere

- Dojo community / martial arts associations
- Email newsletter
- Family recommendation (children/grandchildren install it)

## Persona 3: Le Jeune Urbain

### Profil

- **Archetype**: Young adult discovering martial arts through anime, manga, UFC
- **Age**: 18-25 ans
- **Contexte**: Curious about authentic martial arts beyond entertainment, values diversity and authenticity
- **Digital**: TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Reddit, mobile gaming

### Douleur Principale

Interested in martial arts culture but drowning in superficial content. Wants something authentic, not another fighting game or clickbait.

### Objectif

Discover the real history and philosophy behind martial arts, in an engaging format that respects the source material.

### Objections Courantes

1. "Is this game actually fun or just a lecture?"
2. "The graphics look like they're for kids."
3. "Why would I play this instead of a AAA fighting game?"

### Message Cle

"DOJUKU SHINGI is what you've been looking for: the real stories, the real techniques, the real wisdom. No shortcuts, no BS."

### Canal Prefere

- TikTok/Instagram Reels (short technique clips)
- Reddit (r/martialarts, r/gaming)
- YouTube (cultural deep dives)
